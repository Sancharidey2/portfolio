My Web Development Journey

A look back at my growth and milestones

My journey into web development began with learning HTML, the backbone of every website. I still remember the thrill of writing my first piece of code and watching it come alive in a browser. It was a small step but opened the door to a world of creativity.

Soon after, I moved on to CSS, where I discovered my passion for design. The ability to style and structure webpages in countless ways fascinated me. I experimented with layouts, colors, and animations, transforming simple ideas into elegant designs.

Participating in a hackathon was a turning point in my journey. I had the opportunity to collaborate with peers, tackle real-world challenges, and learn Bootstrap. It made creating responsive and professional websites much more efficient.
